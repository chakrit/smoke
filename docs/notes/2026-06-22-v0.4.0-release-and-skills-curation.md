## The migration-cascade lesson (path-deps)

The fix re-keys **every** lock in the repo, not just `test/tests.lock.yml`: the self-test
drives fixtures under `test/testdata/`, each with its **own** colocated lock keyed under
the old typed path. A basename change makes every fixture lock lookup miss.

- **Stable fixtures** (UNCHANGED-expected: stabletests, cuetests, jsontests, jsonltests,
  jsonctests) → **re-commit** (`smoke --commit`); pure re-key.
- **Intentional-drift fixtures** (badtests, timeouttests) → **key-line-only rename**;
  re-committing erases the baseline drift the self-test asserts, and a global substitution
  corrupts the `test/testdata/badtests.txt` path that lives in their *command values*.
- **Acceptance gate:** `normalize(old)==normalize(new)` (collapse known path prefixes) →
  empty residual proves the only change is the basename collapse, no env/toolchain/network
  noise. Recorded in `docs/notes/2026-06-21-spec-filename-path-dependence.md` (Resolution).
